Social Media Checklist for College Students

✅ Do’s
Think before you post anything online
Use strong passwords and enable privacy settings
Share useful and positive content
Respect others’ opinions and communicate politely
Verify information before sharing (avoid spreading fake news)

❌ Don’ts
Don’t share personal or sensitive information publicly
Don’t engage in cyberbullying or online arguments
Don’t click on unknown or suspicious links
Don’t post inappropriate or offensive content
Don’t waste excessive time on social media
