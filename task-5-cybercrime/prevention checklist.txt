Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist (For College Students in India)

Do’s:

1.Use strong, unique passwords for every account (combine letters, numbers, symbols).
2.Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on email, banking, and social media apps.
3.Verify links before clicking, especially in emails, WhatsApp, or SMS.
4.Keep your phone and apps updated to avoid security vulnerabilities.
5.Use only official apps from Play Store/App Store for payments and banking.
6.Lock your phone and laptop with a PIN, password, or biometric security.
7.Regularly check your bank statements and transaction history.
8.Use secure Wi-Fi; avoid logging into sensitive accounts on public networks.

UPI & Financial Safety Tips:

1.Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or bank details with anyone.
2.Remember: You enter UPI PIN only to send money, not to receive it.
3.Double-check UPI IDs before making payments.
4.Avoid scanning unknown QR codes or accepting random payment requests.

Don’ts:

1.Don’t share personal information (address, ID proofs, college ID) publicly online.
2.Don’t download attachments or apps from unknown sources.
3.Don’t trust “urgent” messages asking for money or account verification.
4.Don’t reuse the same password across multiple accounts.
